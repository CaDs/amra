title: The Arcane Concord
subTitle: A coalition of mages and scholars seeking lost secrets and responsible arcane power.
body:
## Overview

The Arcane Concord is a coalition of mages, scholars, and arcane practitioners from across Amra, united in their quest for knowledge and magical understanding. With the conviction that magic is a vital force that can benefit all, they seek to uncover lost secrets, share their discoveries, and wield arcane power responsibly.
The symbol of the Arcane Concord is an open tome encircled by a shimmering, multi-colored aureole, representing the organization's commitment to knowledge and the vast spectrum of magical energy. Members proudly wear a pendant or amulet bearing this symbol as an emblem of their allegiance.

The Concord is led by a council known as the Synod of Illumination, comprised of the most learned and influential members from each of Amra's regions. They select a leader, known as the Archmage of the Concord, who is responsible for directing the organization's activities and policies. The current Archmage, Maelis Sunstone, is a formidable wizard who seeks to promote unity and understanding among the various magical traditions.
