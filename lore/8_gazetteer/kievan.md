## Overview

Over four centuries past, a band of valiant warriors and intrepid adventurers, weary of strife and yearning for a sanctuary to call home, embarked on a journey to forge a new realm. Guided by the legendary Yaroslav Kievan, a warrior of unmatched prowess, they discovered a secluded valley nestled between towering mountains and began the arduous labor of constructing their haven.

In its infancy, the nascent human kingdom of Kievan found itself beset on all sides by formidable foes: Skapta to the north, Aregor to the east, Tianzhu to the south, and Banlaya to the west. The fledgling nation fought tooth and nail to preserve its territory and uphold its sovereignty. Amidst the chaos, the Drakonov family, then a minor noble house, seized the opportunity to prove their mettle in leadership and warfare, ultimately ascending to power and establishing a dynasty that would endure for generations.

The rulers of Kievan devoted themselves to cultivating a culture steeped in military discipline and martial prowess. They erected formidable fortresses, indoctrinated their citizenry in the arts of war, and adapted their education and religious systems to instill a fierce sense of duty and allegiance to the kingdom. The relentless onslaught of conflicts against neighboring nations tempered the spirit of Kievan's people, molding them into a courageous, dedicated, and fiercely loyal populace.

Yet amidst the unyielding strife, Kievan also became a bastion of innovation and progress. Marvels of engineering and architecture arose within its borders, with grand bridges and aqueducts enhancing the lives of its citizens. The kingdom nurtured trade and agriculture, ensuring that its people remained well-fed and prosperous.

In the present day, Kievan persists as a compact but proud kingdom, ever vigilant in defending its territory and fiercely committed to preserving its independence at any cost. Its citizens take immense pride in their culture of discipline and selflessness, laboring relentlessly to safeguard the future of their kingdom in a world teeming with perils and adversaries.

House Drakonov, now seated upon Kievan's throne, governs with sagacity and unwavering resolve, shepherding its people through tumultuous times and unyielding challenges. Forged in the crucible of warfare and adversity, the heroes and leaders of Kievan stand prepared to confront any menace and wage battle for the survival and prosperity of their beloved realm.

Honoring the legacy of Yaroslav Kievan, the realm's foundation is remembered through annual festivities, revering the sacrifices made to carve out their refuge in the world. These celebrations serve to remind the citizenry of their shared history, keeping the spirit of unity and resilience alive. Storytellers spin tales of Yaroslav's heroism and the Drakonov family's rise to power, ensuring each new generation is imbued with the principles that their forebears stood for.

## Politics

### House Dragonov

Kievan's royal family is the House of Drakonov, a dynasty that has held power for centuries. According to legend, the Drakonov house was founded by Ivan Drakonov, a powerful sorcerer who wiped out the Red Bear tribe from Skapta in the siege of Cotton at a time of great conflict. Since then, the house has ruled Kievan with an Ironfist, maintaining military discipline and the cult of combat as its main values.

The Drakonov are said to have dragon blood running through their veins, giving them special abilities and powers.

House Drakonov has ruled with a firm but fair hand, managing to maintain Kievan's independence in a region rife with conflict. The current Queen of Kievan is Anastasia Drakonov, a charismatic and cunning leader who has managed to keep the peace at a time of growing tension with neighboring kingdoms. His heir is Prince Nikolai Drakonov, a young warrior who has proven his courage in battle and earned the respect of his people.

Despite its long history, House Drakonov has faced various challenges over the centuries, including usurpation attempts and conflicts with other kingdoms.

However, the Drakonov house has also had some controversial members. One of the kings of the house, Boris Drakonov, was known for his cruelty and his reign was marked by repression and violence. Another member of the house, Princess Ana Drakonova, was charged with treason and sentenced to death for her alleged role in a plot to overthrow the king.

Despite these dark episodes, the Drakonov house has continued to rule the kingdom of Kievan, and has managed to maintain power throughout the centuries thanks to its military discipline and skill in combat.

The current King of Kievan is Alexander Drakonov, a seasoned warrior and skillful tactician who has managed to maintain stability in the kingdom at a time of uncertainty and danger, while fighting off invasions from neighboring kingdoms.

While the rule of House Drakonov has been marked with its fair share of conflict and controversy, it is their ability to adapt and persevere that has earned the enduring respect of their subjects. Among the ranks of the royal family, the women have been particularly influential. Grand Duchess Tatiana Drakonova, the sister of King Alexander, is a renowned scholar and diplomat. She is also a popular figure among the citizens, her charitable works and advocacy for education being widely acknowledged and appreciated. Thus, House Drakonov, with all its diverse and remarkable members, continues to lead Kievan into an era of prosperity and unity, standing as a beacon of hope amidst a sea of tumultuous tides.

As a hub of industry, culture, and defense, Boritau is a city with much to offer adventurers and travelers alike. Whether seeking to trade in its high-quality goods, learn from its skilled craftsmen, or explore the mysteries hidden within the mountain, Boritau is a city that should not be missed.

#### Plots and Adventures

The city of Boritau, echoing with the rhythmic clatter of pickaxes against rocks, is a bustling mining hub replete with wealthy mines laden with precious metals and gemstones. These bountiful mines drive the city's economy, but also house dangers deep within their crevices, making the protection of the city an adventurous task for the bravest of heroes.

An intriguing adventure plot beckoning explorers in Boritau could involve them in the investigation of mysterious events plaguing the city's mines. Reports of miners vanishing, bizarre noises, and uncanny movements deep within the mines have sent waves of concern through the city. Tasked with venturing into the mines, the adventurers must brave hostile creatures and solve enigmatic puzzles to unearth the origin of these perturbations.

Alternatively, the adventurers may find themselves tasked with safeguarding a convoy transporting valuable minerals from the mines to the city. In addition to the treacherous terrain and menacing creatures, the threat of bandit ambushes or enemy assaults loom over the journey, demanding vigilance and courage.

Within Boritau's cityscape, adventurers could become entangled in a bitter contention between the human and dwarf communities over mining rights or resource allocation. The adventurers may be entrusted with the delicate task of mediating the dispute and restoring peace, further exacerbating the challenge posed by the city's predicaments.

## Sites

#### Drakonov Castle

The fortress of Drakonov stands as a testament to the might and power of the ruling family of the kingdom of Kievan. Built centuries ago on a rocky peak, the castle has been a symbol of strength and security for the realm ever since. Its towers reach into the clouds, and its thick walls are made of rough-hewn stone, which has withstood the tests of time.

The castle is not only the residence of the royal family but also a garrison for the kingdom's finest warriors. From the battlements, the guards keep a watchful eye over the surrounding lands, always vigilant for signs of potential threats. The castle's armory is said to contain some of the finest weapons and armor in the land, with blades forged by the most skilled smiths and armors made by the most skilled artisans.

The castle's most impressive feature is its keep, a towering structure that dominates the skyline. From its top, one can see for miles, taking in the beauty of the surrounding countryside. The keep is accessible only by a winding staircase, and its interior is full of secrets and surprises.

Despite numerous attempts by enemies to capture the castle, it has never fallen to invaders. It remains a bastion of strength and power, a symbol of the might of the kingdom and the bravery of its people.

#### The Kievan Warrior Academy

The Kievan Warrior Academy stands as a bastion of honor and bravery, where the most elite soldiers of the kingdom are trained to defend their land from all who would seek to do them harm. The academy is situated in a sprawling fortress, with high walls and watchtowers that offer a clear view of the surrounding terrain.

To gain entry into the academy, an aspiring warrior must prove themselves through a series of grueling trials and tests of strength, endurance, and skill. Those who pass these tests are welcomed into the ranks of the academy, where they begin a rigorous training program that lasts for years.

Under the guidance of experienced warriors, students learn the art of combat, mastering a wide range of weapons and tactics. They are taught to work together as a cohesive unit, learning to anticipate each other's movements and respond quickly to changing battle conditions. In addition to physical training, students also learn military strategy, studying the great battles of history and learning to apply those lessons to modern warfare.

The academy is also home to a vast library, containing priceless tomes on the art of war, history, and military strategy. Here, students can immerse themselves in the wisdom of the ages, learning from the greatest minds in the kingdom's history.

Upon graduation, the warriors of the academy are highly respected and revered, considered the best of the best. They are entrusted with the kingdom's most important missions, and their bravery and skill are instrumental in defending Kievan from its enemies.

#### The Eastern Citadel

The Eastern Citadel is a testament to Kievan's strength and resilience, built on the mountains that separate the kingdom from its restless eastern neighbor, Aregor. The fortress is a formidable sight to behold, with an imposing stone wall that stretches as far as the eye can see, a towering keep that pierces the sky, and a deep moat that surrounds the entire structure. It is said that the Eastern Citadel was built centuries ago, in response to Aregor's aggressive raids, and has withstood numerous attacks over the years.

Inside the fortress walls, Kievan's best-trained soldiers can be found, standing guard and keeping watch for any signs of enemy incursion. These soldiers are known for their military discipline, expert combat skills, and fierce loyalty to their kingdom. The Eastern Citadel is also home to a great armory, where weapons and armor of the highest quality are crafted by the most skilled blacksmiths in the realm.

Beneath the imposing structures of the Eastern Citadel, a network of labyrinthine tunnels and secret passages extends into the heart of the mountains. These passages are as much a part of the Citadel's defensive architecture as the walls and towers that mark its exterior. Meticulously crafted by skilled dwarven masons, the tunnels provide secret exit routes in times of siege, ensuring the continuous flow of supplies and serving as discreet conduits for intelligence operatives. They also harbor hidden armories, safeguarding additional caches of weapons and provisions for protracted battles. This elaborate subterranean labyrinth, known only to a trusted few, adds an additional layer of security and strategy, further enhancing the Citadel's defensive prowess. The Eastern Citadel, thus, stands not just as a bulwark against external threats, but as an embodiment of Kievan's astuteness, resilience, and the indomitable spirit of its people.

#### The Dragon's Maw

Deep within the mountain ranges of Kievan lies a cave that has captivated the imagination of adventurers for centuries - the Dragon's Maw. Legend has it that the cave was once home to a ferocious dragon, who terrorized the surrounding countryside before being vanquished by a legendary hero. However, tales abound that there is still an untold wealth of treasures hidden within the cave's twisting passages.

Getting to the Dragon's Maw is a dangerous journey, requiring a trek through treacherous terrain and climbing up steep cliffs. The entrance to the cave itself is hidden behind a waterfall, and the only way in is through a narrow, winding tunnel. Once inside, adventurers are faced with a daunting labyrinth of passages and tunnels, with no clear path to the treasures hidden within.

The Dragon's Maw is a deadly place, full of traps, hidden crevices, and deadly monsters. However, it is also rumored to contain treasures beyond imagination - ancient artifacts, magical weapons, and piles of gold and jewels. Many adventurers have attempted to find the treasure and failed, never to return again. Still, those who succeed in braving the cave's dangers are celebrated as heroes, and the treasures they bring back are the stuff of legends.

For some Kievan clans, the Dragon's Maw is a sacred place, and the dragon that once resided there is revered as a symbol of strength and wisdom. These clans sometimes send their bravest warriors to the cave to pay homage to the dragon and seek its blessings. But for others, the cave is a source of greed and temptation, an irresistible challenge to test their mettle and acquire unimaginable riches. Whatever the motivation, the Dragon's Maw remains a place of great fascination and danger for all who dare to venture within its depths.

#### The South Market

The South Market of Kievan is an adventure in and of itself. Its labyrinthine alleys, haphazard stalls, and countless wares make it a hub of commerce and activity. The many aromas of exotic spices, fine textiles, and freshly cooked foods mingle with the bustling sounds of people haggling over prices and traders announcing their wares.

But beneath the bright facade of the market, a darker underbelly lurks. It is whispered that the market is the place to find illicit goods, such as dangerous alchemical substances, drugs, and black powder. These items are not only forbidden but also highly dangerous, and their possession can lead to a swift and severe punishment.

Unscrupulous merchants and shady characters work to provide these forbidden items to those who seek them, and the watchful eyes of the law can be easily evaded in the chaos of the market.

Despite the risks, the allure of the forbidden is strong, and many adventurers and thrill-seekers are drawn to the South Market to see what they can find. However, they must be careful, for the consequences of getting caught with unlawful goods are dire.

But for those who seek only legitimate goods, the South Market offers a plethora of options, from fine jewelry and handcrafted objects to sturdy armor and deadly weapons of war. The craftsmen and jewelers of the market are highly skilled and revered, and their wares are sought after throughout the kingdom.

The South Market is a place of contrasts, a thriving hub of commerce and culture that also hides dangerous secrets. It is a place where fortunes can be made or lost, and where danger and adventure lurk around every corner.

#### The Watchtower

The Watchtower stands tall and proud on a hill on the outskirts of Kievan, overlooking the borderlands of the kingdom. For centuries, it has stood as a symbol of Kievan's commitment to protecting its people and lands from outside threats. Built with a narrow and imposing design, the tower has been continuously updated with the latest in defensive and surveillance technologies. The tower's arsenal includes telescopes to spot incoming danger from afar, short-range guns to ward off potential invaders, and other detection devices to alert the soldiers in the tower of any threat.

In addition to its defensive capabilities, the Watchtower holds great significance to the people of Kievan. It serves as a meeting place for the kingdom's leaders, where important decisions are made and traditions are honored. The tower's interior is adorned with murals and tapestries depicting the history and heroes of the kingdom, and relics of ancient battles are proudly displayed for all to see.

Those who brave the climb to the top of the Watchtower are rewarded with a breathtaking view of the surrounding landscape. From the peak of the tower, one can see the kingdom's mountains, valleys, and forests stretching out as far as the eye can see. On clear days, the sea can be seen on the distant horizon, reminding all who stand at the tower's pinnacle of the dangers that lurk beyond the kingdom's borders.

The Watchtower is a place of great importance to the kingdom, a symbol of its strength and determination in the face of adversity. Visitors to Kievan cannot leave without paying their respects to this iconic structure, and marveling at its beauty and power.

#### The Wolf's Pass

Wolf's Pass is a treacherous path through the mountains, surrounded by steep cliffs and perilous gorges. It is a key strategic location for the Kievan kingdom's defenses, and as such, has been fortified with a series of formidable forts and checkpoints manned by the kingdom's most experienced warriors. The pass has been the site of countless battles throughout history, with many legendary heroes and villains meeting their fate within its treacherous confines. The blood of these brave warriors is said to have soaked into the very rocks and soil of the pass, imbuing it with a dark and foreboding power.

Despite the dangers, some adventurous souls still seek to travel through Wolf's Pass, drawn by the promise of riches and the thrill of perilous adventure. Many have perished in the attempt, their bones left to bleach in the unforgiving sun or be swept away by the howling winds. Others have emerged victorious, their pockets full of gold and their hearts filled with tales of bravery and daring.

As travelers make their way through Wolf's Pass, they must navigate treacherous terrain, including crumbling paths, narrow bridges, and steep cliffs. Ferocious beasts lurk in the shadows, ready to pounce on unsuspecting travelers at any moment. But for those who survive the dangers and emerge on the other side, the rewards can be great indeed. Legends speak of vast treasure troves hidden deep within the mountains, waiting for those brave enough to claim them.

Within Wolf's Pass, one can also find scattered traces of ancient civilizations, their ruins and artifacts obscured by the unforgiving harshness of time. Mysterious carvings and strange symbols etched into the stone walls hint at a history long lost, sparking fascination and curiosity among the scholars and adventurers alike. There are whispered stories of ancient vaults and crypts embedded within the depths of the mountains, protected by riddles and traps of olden magic. Many fortune seekers have ventured off the path in pursuit of these legends, their lust for lost riches often leading them into deadly peril, only to add to the haunted history of the pass.

Over the centuries, Wolf's Pass has grown to represent a stark contrast, a symbol of both reckless daring and overwhelming dread. Its beauty is undeniable as the setting sun paints the steep cliffs with hues of gold and crimson, creating a scene of breathtaking splendor. The serene silence of its nights, occasionally broken by the distant howl of a lone wolf, invokes a sense of solitude and eerie calm. Yet, this enchanting allure can swiftly turn into an icy grip of fear when the pass's treacherous inhabitants emerge from their hideouts, their menacing shadows casting long dark silhouettes under the moonlight. Despite its dangers, the allure of Wolf's Pass persists, a siren call to adventurers, historians, and treasure seekers who dare to brave its formidable reputation in the pursuit of glory and riches.
